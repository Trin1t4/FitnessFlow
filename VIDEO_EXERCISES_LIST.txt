## BODYWEIGHT - LOWER BODY (12)

### 1. Pause Squat
**Setup:** Feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly out, arms extended forward
**Cues:**
- Descend to bottom position
- Hold 2-3 seconds at bottom
- Maintain tension throughout pause
- Keep chest up during pause
- Drive up explosively after pause
**Avoid:** Relaxing at bottom, bouncing out, losing tension

---

### 2. Squat Jump
**Setup:** Feet shoulder-width, arms at sides, athletic stance
**Cues:**
- Descend into quarter/half squat
- Explode upward, fully extend
- Swing arms for momentum
- Land softly, absorb impact
- Reset between reps
**Avoid:** Landing stiff-legged, shallow squat, not fully extending

---

### 3. Split Squat
**Setup:** Staggered stance, feet hip-width apart, torso upright
**Cues:**
- Lower straight down
- Back knee toward floor
- Front knee tracks over toes
- Keep torso vertical
- Drive through front heel
**Avoid:** Leaning forward, front knee caving, short range of motion

---

### 4. Reverse Lunge
**Setup:** Standing tall, feet together, hands on hips or at sides
**Cues:**
- Step backward with one leg
- Lower back knee toward floor
- Front shin stays vertical
- Push through front foot to return
- Alternate legs
**Avoid:** Stepping too short, torso leaning, knee touching floor hard

---

### 5. Skater Squat
**Setup:** Stand on one leg, other leg behind, arms forward for balance
**Cues:**
- Hinge at hips slightly
- Lower back knee toward floor
- Keep standing foot flat
- Drive up through heel
- Control the descent
**Avoid:** Letting back foot touch, losing balance, torso collapsing

---

### 6. Shrimp Squat
**Setup:** Stand on one leg, grab opposite foot behind with hand
**Cues:**
- Lower back knee to floor
- Keep chest up
- Hold foot throughout
- Touch knee gently
- Drive up to standing
**Avoid:** Releasing foot, falling forward, rushing the movement

---

### 7. Single Leg Glute Bridge
**Setup:** Lie on back, one knee bent, other leg extended, arms at sides
**Cues:**
- Drive through planted heel
- Lift hips until body is straight
- Keep extended leg in line
- Squeeze glute at top
- Lower with control
**Avoid:** Rotating hips, hyperextending back, dropping extended leg

---

### 8. Single Leg RDL
**Setup:** Stand on one leg, slight knee bend, arms hanging
**Cues:**
- Hinge at hips, not waist
- Extend free leg behind
- Reach toward floor
- Keep back flat
- Return using hamstring/glute
**Avoid:** Rounding back, bending standing knee too much, rotating hips

---

### 9. Elevated Hip Thrust
**Setup:** Upper back on bench, one or both feet on floor, hips below bench level
**Cues:**
- Drive through heels
- Lift hips to full extension
- Squeeze glutes hard at top
- Keep chin tucked
- Lower with control
**Avoid:** Hyperextending lower back, pushing through toes, incomplete lockout

---

### 10. Slider Leg Curl
**Setup:** Lie on back, heels on sliders/towel, hips lifted
**Cues:**
- Keep hips elevated throughout
- Slide heels away from body
- Control the extension
- Curl heels back toward glutes
- Maintain hip height
**Avoid:** Dropping hips, rushing, losing control at end range

---

### 11. Box Squat
**Setup:** Box/bench behind, feet shoulder-width, arms forward
**Cues:**
- Sit back onto box
- Pause briefly on box
- Keep shins vertical
- Drive up without rocking
- Control the descent
**Avoid:** Plopping down, rocking forward, bouncing off box

---

### 12. Assisted Pistol Squat
**Setup:** Stand on one leg, hold TRX/doorframe/pole for support
**Cues:**
- Extend free leg forward
- Lower slowly to bottom
- Use minimal assistance
- Keep heel planted
- Drive up through heel
**Avoid:** Relying too much on support, heel lifting, collapsing at bottom

## BODYWEIGHT - UPPER PUSH (8)

### 13. Archer Push-up
**Setup:** Wide push-up position, one arm extended to side
**Cues:**
- Lower toward bent arm side
- Straight arm stays extended
- Chest to floor on working side
- Push back to center
- Alternate sides
**Avoid:** Rotating torso, bending extended arm, partial range

---

### 14. Narrow Push-up
**Setup:** Hands closer than shoulder-width, body straight
**Cues:**
- Keep elbows close to body
- Lower chest between hands
- Elbows point backward
- Full range of motion
- Core stays tight
**Avoid:** Flaring elbows, sagging hips, partial reps

---

### 15. Ring Push-up
**Setup:** Hands on rings, body straight, rings at chest height
**Cues:**
- Stabilize rings throughout
- Lower chest to ring level
- Turn rings out at top
- Control the instability
- Keep core engaged
**Avoid:** Letting rings wobble, flaring elbows, rushing

---

### 16. Typewriter Push-up
**Setup:** Wide push-up position, body straight
**Cues:**
- Lower to one side
- Shift horizontally at bottom
- Move to other side
- Push back up
- Keep chest close to floor during shift
**Avoid:** Rising during shift, rushing, losing form

---

### 17. Elevated Pike Push-up
**Setup:** Feet on box/bench, hips high, hands on floor
**Cues:**
- Form inverted V shape
- Lower head toward floor
- Elbows bend to sides
- Head between hands at bottom
- Push through shoulders
**Avoid:** Flattening out, kicking off box, insufficient pike

---

### 18. Handstand Hold
**Setup:** Kick up to wall, hands shoulder-width, fingers spread
**Cues:**
- Stack joints vertically
- Squeeze core tight
- Push through shoulders
- Look at floor between hands
- Breathe steadily
**Avoid:** Banana back, collapsing shoulders, holding breath

---

### 19. Pseudo Planche Push-up
**Setup:** Push-up position, hands rotated outward/backward, lean forward
**Cues:**
- Shift weight forward over hands
- Keep elbows close to body
- Lower chest toward floor
- Maintain forward lean
- Push back up
**Avoid:** Not leaning enough, elbows flaring, losing lean at bottom

---

### 20. Clap Push-up
**Setup:** Standard push-up position, body tight
**Cues:**
- Lower with control
- Explode upward
- Clap hands at peak
- Land softly on hands
- Absorb into next rep
**Avoid:** Partial push-up, late clap, hard landing

## BODYWEIGHT - CORE (6)

### 27. Copenhagen Plank

**Avoid:** Hips dropping, rotating, holding breath

---

### 28. Lying Leg Raise

**Cues:**
- Keep lower back pressed down
- Lift legs together
- Stop at 90 degrees or less
- Lower with control
- Don't let feet touch floor
**Avoid:** Arching back, using momentum, bending knees

---

### 29. L-Sit
**Setup:** Hands on parallettes/floor, legs extended forward
**Cues:**
- Lock arms straight
- Lift legs parallel to floor
- Point toes
- Depress shoulders

---

### 30. Dragon Flag
**Setup:** Lie on bench, grip behind head, body straight
**Cues:**
- Roll up onto shoulders
- Keep body rigid as one unit
- Lower slowly, controlled

**Avoid:** Bending at hips, dropping too fast, using momentum

## GYM - TEST POTENZA (7)

### 31. Counter Movement Jump (CMJ)
**Setup:** Stand tall, feet hip-width, hands on hips
**Cues:**
- Quick dip into quarter squat
- Explode upward immediately
- Full hip/knee/ankle extension
- Land softly, absorb
- Hands stay on hips throughout
**Avoid:** Pausing at bottom, arm swing, stiff landing

---

### 32. Squat Jump (Test)
**Setup:** Start in half squat position, hands on hips, hold 3 seconds
**Cues:**
- No countermovement
- Explode from static position
- Full extension at top
- Land softly
- Compare to CMJ for reactive strength
**Avoid:** Bouncing before jump, using arms, not holding start

---

### 33. Drop Jump
**Setup:** Stand on box 30-40cm, step off (don't jump)
**Cues:**
- Step off, don't jump down
- Land on both feet
- Immediately rebound upward
- Minimize ground contact time
- Land softly on second landing
**Avoid:** Jumping off box, slow transition, collapsing on land

---

### 34. Broad Jump
**Setup:** Toes behind line, feet hip-width, arms ready
**Cues:**
- Swing arms back
- Explode forward and up
- Drive arms forward
- Extend fully at takeoff
- Land and stick
**Avoid:** Stepping over line, not using arms, falling backward

---

### 35. Box Jump
**Setup:** Face box, athletic stance, arms ready
**Cues:**
- Swing arms and dip
- Explode upward
- Tuck knees to clear box
- Land softly on box
- Stand fully, step down
**Avoid:** Landing in deep squat, jumping down, not extending

---

### 36. Med Ball Chest Pass
**Setup:** Stand facing wall, med ball at chest
**Cues:**
- Load by bringing ball to chest
- Explode through arms
- Release at full extension
- Follow through
- Catch and repeat
**Avoid:** Using legs too much, short arms, not following through

---

### 37. Med Ball Overhead Throw
**Setup:** Stand facing direction of throw, ball overhead
**Cues:**
- Load behind head
- Drive through core and arms
- Release at optimal angle
- Follow through completely
- Full body extension
**Avoid:** Throwing flat, not using core, early release

---

## GYM - TEST FMS (6)

### 38. FMS Deep Squat
**Setup:** Feet shoulder-width, toes forward, dowel overhead
**Cues:**
- Keep dowel overhead, arms locked
- Squat as deep as possible
- Heels stay down
- Knees track over toes
- Torso stays upright
**Avoid:** Heels rising, dowel falling forward, knees caving

---

### 39. FMS Hurdle Step
**Setup:** Stand with toes at hurdle, dowel across shoulders
**Cues:**
- Lift leg over hurdle
- Keep stance leg stable
- Touch heel on other side
- Return to start
- Maintain upright posture
**Avoid:** Leaning, rotating hips, touching hurdle

---

### 40. FMS Inline Lunge
**Setup:** Feet on line, front heel at end, back toes at mark, dowel on back
**Cues:**
- Lower back knee to board
- Keep dowel contact (head, back, sacrum)
- Return to standing
- Maintain balance
- Stay on the line
**Avoid:** Losing dowel contact, stepping off line, losing balance

---

### 41. FMS Active Straight Leg Raise
**Setup:** Lie supine, legs straight, arms at sides
**Cues:**
- Keep non-moving leg flat
- Raise one leg as high as possible
- Keep knee straight
- Note position relative to landmarks
- Lower with control
**Avoid:** Bending either knee, lifting opposite leg, rotating

---

### 42. FMS Trunk Stability Push-up
**Setup:** Prone, hands at forehead (men) or chin (women) level
**Cues:**
- Perform push-up with body as unit
- No spinal movement
- Chest and hips rise together
- Full lockout at top
- Body stays rigid
**Avoid:** Sagging, piking, segmental movement

---

### 43. FMS Rotary Stability
**Setup:** Quadruped position, hands under shoulders, knees under hips
**Cues:**
- Extend same-side arm and leg
- Touch elbow to knee under body
- Return to extension
- Keep back flat throughout
- Minimal rotation
**Avoid:** Rotating trunk, losing balance, arching back

# VIDEO PRODUCTION NOTES

## General Guidelines:
1. **Camera angles:** Front, side, and 45° for each exercise
2. **Demonstrate:** Correct form first, then common mistakes
3. **Tempo:** Show controlled tempo (2-1-2 typical)
4. **Breathing:** Visible breathing pattern when possible
5. **Range of motion:** Full ROM clearly visible
6. **Attire:** Form-fitting clothes to see muscle engagement

## Technical Specs:
- **Resolution:** 1080p (1920x1080)
- **Frame rate:** 30fps
- **Duration:** 10-20 seconds per exercise (loop)
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Background:** Neutral, good lighting
- **Naming:** exercise-name-lowercase.mp4
